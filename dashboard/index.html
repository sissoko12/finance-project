FORMULAS & METHODOLOGY

Efficiency Ratio

Noninterest Expense / Net Revenue × 100%

Lower is better. Measures cost to generate each dollar of revenue. Typical range: 50–70% for US banks.

Net Interest Margin (NIM)

Net Interest Income / Average Earning Assets × 100%

Core banking profitability metric. Range ~1.5–3.5% for large US banks; Schwab and custody banks tend lower.

Return on Equity (ROE)

Net Income / Average Stockholders Equity × 100%

Measures profit generated on shareholders' capital. Target ~10–15% for large banks.

Return on Assets (ROA)

Net Income / Average Total Assets × 100%

Measures how efficiently assets generate profit. Well-run US banks typically target ~1%.

CET1 Capital Ratio

Common Equity Tier 1 / Risk-Weighted Assets × 100%

Basel III core capital measure (post-2013). US G-SIB minimum ~11.5–12.5%. Higher = stronger capital position.

Tier 1 Capital Ratio

Tier 1 Capital / Risk-Weighted Assets × 100%

Regulatory capital divided by risk-adjusted assets. Basel II minimum: 4%, Basel III: 6%.

Net Charge-off Rate

(Gross Charge-offs − Recoveries) / Average Net Loans × 100%

Portion of loan portfolio written off as uncollectible. Typically <1% in normal times; spiked 2–4%+ in 2009–2010.

NPL Ratio

Nonperforming Loans / Total Loans × 100%

Loans 90+ days past due or on non-accrual. Rose sharply during GFC; key early warning indicator.

Allowance Coverage Ratio

Allowance for Loan Losses / Nonperforming Loans × 100%

How much of NPLs are covered by reserves. Above 100% = more than fully covered.

Pre-Provision Profit

Net Revenue − Total Noninterest Expense

Earnings before credit loss provisions. Shows underlying business profitability independent of credit cycle.

MD&A Sentiment Score

(Positive words − Negative words) / Total words

Applied to Item 7 (MD&A) section. Uses a domain-specific financial word list. Negative scores dominated the 2008–2009 period across all banks.

Data Sources

All data extracted from SEC EDGAR 10-K annual filings. XBRL (inline) extraction used for filings post-2009; HTML table extraction for earlier filings. Pre-2009 quality is lower — no XBRL tags available.

Data Coverage Notes

WFC and USB: EDGAR delivered a stub/schema file for some recent years — financial tables not available in those filings as downloaded. BK data begins 2007 (post-merger with Mellon). CET1 ratio null before 2013 is correct (introduced in Basel III).
